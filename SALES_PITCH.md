# Sales Pitch

## Who This Is For

- Small finance teams drowning in invoice exceptions.
- Founders who need AP to look more mature without hiring more headcount.
- Controllers who care about auditability, not just automation.

## The One-Line Pitch

This product turns invoice review from a manual bottleneck into a fast, auditable approval flow that catches bad invoices before they hit the ERP.

## The Buyer Problem

Most AP teams are not asking for more software. They are asking for fewer surprises:

- invoices with bad totals
- missing PO numbers
- duplicate submissions
- approvals that cannot be audited cleanly
- exports that require cleanup before import

## The Outcome You Sell

- Less manual checking.
- Faster invoice approvals.
- Clear exception reasons.
- Audit history on every edit and decision.
- CSV export that is ready to hand off.

## 30-Second Pitch

"We help AP teams stop wasting time on invoice cleanup. You upload PDFs, the system extracts and validates the data, flags exceptions, and lets you approve or correct invoices with a full audit trail. Approved invoices export cleanly to CSV, so your team spends less time fixing errors and more time closing books."

## Demo Story That Sells

1. Show a normal invoice getting processed.
2. Show an exception invoice with a clear reason.
3. Show a field correction and approval.
4. Show the audit trail.
5. Show the CSV export.

## Why This Wins

- It reduces rework before data reaches the ERP.
- It gives finance a review queue instead of a spreadsheet mess.
- It creates confidence through auditability.
- It is easier to understand than a generic "AI platform" pitch.

## Objection Handling

### "We already have manual review"

That is exactly the problem. The product removes the repetitive part of review and keeps the exception handling.

### "We already have OCR"

OCR is only one step. The value is validation, review, audit trail, and export.

### "Our team is too small for this"

Small teams benefit the most because every manual invoice touch is expensive.

### "Can it connect to our ERP?"

For the MVP, the answer is CSV export. That is enough to prove value before integration work.

## Pilot Offer

Offer a 2-week pilot:

- load 10 to 20 sample invoices
- show exception reduction and approval speed
- measure time saved per invoice
- export approved invoices to CSV

## Metrics To Track In The Pilot

- invoices processed per hour
- manual touches per invoice
- exception rate
- approval turnaround time
- export cleanup time

## What To Avoid Saying

- Do not lead with model names or OCR vendors.
- Do not explain the database first.
- Do not overclaim full automation.
- Do not say "AI" without tying it to a business outcome.

## Better Language

- "Cuts review time."
- "Makes exceptions obvious."
- "Keeps approvals auditable."
- "Exports cleanly."
- "Designed for finance teams."
